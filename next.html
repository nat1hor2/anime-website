Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter is about a boy, Gon, who wants to become a hunter because his dad left him to become one. Gon tries to find his dad and runs into several adventures while training and stuff. Gon reminds me a lot of Naruto because he has incredible percistence and self dicipline. Would definetly recommend.

Attack on Titan

In this world, the human race has almost gone extinct when for some unknown reason titans appear and try to devour all humans. What little is left of the human race now lives inside three walls. One fateful day after hundreds of years of peace there is a titan attack and the outer wall has to be abandoned. After this attack the protagonist Eren's resolve to join the Survey Corps which is an elite military organisation dedicated to exploring the area outside of the walls and trying to fight against the titans. This is a must watch

Naruto

Naruto is an orphan who's parents died when the Nine Tailed Fox atacked the Village Hidden in the Leaves. The monster was huge, and the fourth Hokage (the ruler and strongest Ninja) sacrificed himself to defeat the monster. Even then, they couldn't kill it, but they sealed it inside Naruto, still a baby at this time. Even though Naruto has done nothing wrong, everyone hates him like a monster. Although Naruto is shattered because of how people treat him, he decides only to train harder because of this. If you watch anime, this is something you have to see.

One Punch Man

The protagonist of this story, Saitama starts off as a completely normal human being, and just got fired from his office job. As he is walking back home after job hunting, he encounters a crab-monster trying to kill a child (monsters are a common occurence in this world). Saitama just barely defeats this monster, but after this day he decides to become a hero. He trains incredibly hard and in just three years he becomes an unbeliveably strong superhero. Saitama meets Genos, who wants to become Saitama's diciple after fighting him and losing miserably. Together they go through many adventures, and become stronger together. Would definetly recommend, is kinda comedic bruh
